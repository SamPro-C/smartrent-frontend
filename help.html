Account Access

How do I log in?
Enter your registered email/phone number and password on the login screen.
I forgot my password.
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
I can't access my account.
Contact your landlord if you're a tenant or worker. Landlords should contact system support.

Registration

How do I register?
  • Landlords can register directly through the app.
  • Tenants and workers must be registered by their landlord before they can access the system.
What information is needed for registration?
Full name, contact information, ID, apartment details, and other role-specific information.

Property Management

How do I add a new apartment?
Go to "Properties" ‚Üí "Add New" and complete the required fields.
How do I manage apartment units?
Select an apartment from your dashboard, then use the "Units" tab to add, edit, or remove units.
How do I mark rooms as vacant or occupied?
From the apartment details page, select the unit and room, then use the status toggle.

Tenant Management

How do I register a new tenant?
Go to "Tenants" ‚Üí "Add New" and complete the registration form.
How do I edit tenant information?
Search for the tenant using their ID, then click "Edit" on their profile.
How do I remove a tenant who has moved out?
Search for the tenant, view their profile, and click "Remove Tenant."

Worker Management

How do I add workers?
Go to "Workers" ‚Üí "Add New" and complete the worker registration form.
How do I assign workers to specific apartments?
From the worker's profile, use the "Assignments" tab to select apartments and set schedules.
How do I view worker performance?
Check the "Reports" section and filter by worker name.

Payment Tracking

How do I view payment history?
Go to "Payments" ‚Üí "History" and use filters to view by date, apartment, or tenant.
How do I add a manual payment?
Select "Payments" ‚Üí "Add Manual Payment" and fill in all required details including M-Pesa code if applicable.
How do I generate payment reports?
Go to "Reports" ‚Üí "Payments" and use the filter options to customize your report.

Service Requests

How do I view tenant service requests?
Check the "Service Requests" section of your dashboard.
How do I assign workers to requests?
Open the request and use the "Assign Worker" option.
How do I track request status?
All requests show current status (New, In Progress, Completed).

Notifications

How do I send notifications to tenants?
Go to "Notifications" ‚Üí "New Notification" and select your target audience (all tenants, specific apartment, payment status).
How do I view notification history?
Check the "Notifications" ‚Üí "History" section.

Payments

How do I pay my rent?
Click the "Pay Now" button on your dashboard and follow the prompts.
What payment methods are available?
M-Pesa, bank transfer, and other methods configured by your landlord.
How do I view my payment history?
Check the "Payment History" section on your dashboard.

Service Requests

How do I submit a service request?
Click "Request Service" on your dashboard, complete the form, and attach photos/videos if needed.
How do I check the status of my request?
View the "My Requests" section to see all requests and their current status.
Can I cancel a service request?
Yes, if the status is still "New," you can cancel it from the request details page.

Workers Information

How do I view worker information?
Check the "Workers" section on your dashboard to see workers assigned to your apartment.
How do I contact a worker?
Worker contact details are available in the "Workers" section.

Shopping

How do I access the shopping section?
Click the "Shop" button on your dashboard.
How do I place an order?
Browse products, add items to your cart, and follow the checkout process.
How do I track my order?
Go to "Orders" in the shopping section to view status.

Assignments

How do I view my assigned apartments?
Check the "My Assignments" section on your dashboard.
How do I view my work schedule?
Your schedule is displayed on your dashboard and can be viewed in calendar format.

Service Requests

How do I view service requests assigned to me?
Check the "My Tasks" section on your dashboard.
How do I update the status of a request?
Open the request details and use the "Update Status" option.
How do I communicate with tenants about requests?
Use the messaging feature within each service request.

Common Issues

The app is running slowly.
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.
I can't upload photos/videos.
Check that your files are under the size limit (10MB) and in a supported format.
The page isn't loading correctly.
Try using a different browser or device.

Contact Support

For tenants and workers:
Please contact your landlord for assistance.
For landlords:
Email support@smartrent.com or call our helpline at [support phone number].

Privacy & Security

How is my data protected?
SmartRent uses encryption to protect all personal and payment information.
Who can see my information?
Access is strictly controlled based on user roles:
  • Landlords can only see information related to their properties and users
  • Tenants can only see their own information
  • Workers can only see information needed for their assignments
How do I report a security concern?
Contact support immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.
